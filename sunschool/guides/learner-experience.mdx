## Learner home

The learner home page is the main hub. From here, children can:

- **See their current lesson** — The active lesson card shows the subject and a "Let's Go!" button
- **Pick a subject** — Browse available subjects to generate new lessons
- **View goals** — See reward goals they're saving toward
- **Check progress** — View completed lessons, scores, and achievements

## Lessons

Each lesson is AI-generated and tailored to the child's grade level. Lessons include:

- **Multiple sections** — Key concepts, examples, and practice, each in an expandable accordion
- **SVG illustrations** — Educational graphics generated by AI that match the lesson topic
- **Diagrams** — Flowcharts, concept maps, timelines, and other visual aids
- **Grade-appropriate language** — Vocabulary and sentence complexity matched to the child's level

Lessons are generated on demand. The text content appears immediately, and illustrations load in the background within a few seconds.

## Quizzes

After reading a lesson, children take a quiz:

1. **3 multiple-choice questions** — Each with 4 answer options
2. **Double-or-nothing wager** (optional) — Risk earned points for a chance to double them
3. **Immediate feedback** — See which answers were correct
4. **Points earned** — Correct answers earn points toward rewards

Quizzes test comprehension of the specific lesson content. Questions include concept tags for mastery tracking.

## Points and rewards

Children earn points from quizzes and achievements. Points can be:

- **Saved toward goals** — Set a goal for a specific reward and watch progress
- **Redeemed for rewards** — Request a reward from the parent-managed shop
- **Tracked in history** — Full ledger of points earned and spent

## Achievements

Achievements are awarded automatically for milestones:

- First lesson completed
- Perfect quiz score
- Learning streaks (consecutive days)
- Subject-specific milestones

Each achievement can be shared via a public link.
