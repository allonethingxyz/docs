## Dashboard

After logging in, the parent dashboard shows all your children at a glance — lesson counts, average scores, and quick-launch buttons.

### Add a child

1. Click **Add Child** from the dashboard
2. Enter their name and grade level
3. The child account is created immediately — no separate sign-up needed

Each child gets their own adaptive learning path. You can manage multiple children from one account.

### Start a learning session

Click **Start Learning as [Child]** on any child card. This switches you to learner mode where you see exactly what your child sees.

## Learner mode

Parents can switch between **Grown-Up mode** (dashboard, reports, rewards) and **Learner mode** (lessons, quizzes, progress) using the mode toggle in the header.

In learner mode, you experience the app as your child would — same lessons, same quizzes, same interface. This lets you verify what they're learning.

## Managing subjects

Navigate to **Learners** to manage each child's:

- **Grade level** — Controls the difficulty and vocabulary of AI-generated lessons
- **Subjects** — Add or remove subjects (Math, Science, English, History, and more)
- **Knowledge graph** — Visual map of concepts the child has studied

## Reports

The Reports page shows per-child analytics:

- Lessons completed and scores over time
- Concept mastery breakdown (which topics they're strong or weak in)
- Achievement history
- PDF export for record-keeping

## Rewards

Parents manage a rewards shop that children can spend their earned points in.

### Create rewards

1. Go to **Rewards** from the dashboard
2. Click **Create Reward** and set a title, description, point cost, and optional emoji
3. Rewards appear in your child's goals page

### Approve redemptions

When a child requests a reward redemption:

1. You'll see it in the **Pending Redemptions** list
2. Review and **Approve** or **Reject** with optional notes
3. Points are deducted on approval

## Double-or-nothing

Each quiz question offers an optional **double-or-nothing** wager. Children can risk their earned points for a chance to double them. Parents can enable or disable this feature per child in learner settings.

## Data sync

For advanced users, Sunschool supports syncing your family's data to an external PostgreSQL database. Find this under **Database Sync** in the dashboard.
