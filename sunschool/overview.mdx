Sunschool is an open source AI tutor that meets your kid where they are — grade level, learning style, speed. Every lesson adapts in real time. No two kids get the same experience.

## What kids experience

- **Lessons that feel like theirs** — The AI adjusts to how your child actually learns, not a grade-wide average.
- **Play, not homework** — Quizzes feel like games. Challenges feel like puzzles.
- **Trophies and streaks** — Badges and milestones keep momentum going.
- **SVG illustrations** — Every lesson includes AI-generated educational graphics.

## What parents get

- **See everything, in real time** — Live progress on strengths, gaps, and growth.
- **Your data stays yours** — Nothing gets sold. Nothing trains a model. Export or delete anytime.
- **All your kids, one account** — Each child gets their own adaptive path.
- **Open source, all the way down** — Read the code. Audit the prompts. Self-host if you want.

## The parent owns the prompt

AI models are sensitive to steering. Small changes in a prompt can shift tone, difficulty, and emphasis in ways that aren't obvious from the output alone.

If you don't know what's in the prompt, you don't really know what's in the lesson. Sunschool makes every prompt visible — before and after the lesson.
